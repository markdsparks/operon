# Refund policy

Regular-price merchandise may be returned within 30 calendar days of purchase
when the customer provides the original receipt and the merchandise is unopened.

Discounted and clearance merchandise is final sale and cannot be returned,
even when it is unopened and the customer has a receipt.

Defective merchandise may be exchanged for the same item within 90 days. A
manager may approve a refund only when a replacement is unavailable.

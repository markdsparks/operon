# Paid leave policy

Bereavement leave provides five paid workdays for an immediate family member
and three paid workdays for an extended family member. Parents, children,
spouses, and siblings are immediate family. Grandparents, grandchildren, aunts,
uncles, nieces, and nephews are extended family. Pet loss is not covered.

Parental leave provides 12 paid weeks when the employee has completed 12 months
of service by the date of birth or adoption. Employees with less than 12 months
of service receive six paid weeks.

# Production access policy

Permanent employees may receive production administrator access only after
completing annual security training and registering a hardware security key.
Both conditions are mandatory.

Contractors and temporary workers may not receive production administrator
access under the standard process, even if they complete training and possess a
hardware key.

During a declared incident, the incident commander may authorize break-glass
administrator access for any named responder. Break-glass access expires after
60 minutes and every action is audited.
